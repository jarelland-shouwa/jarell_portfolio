As part of the SST-NP IDP, I participated in an innovation camp in June 2023. Beforehand, I visited the Advanced Remanufacturing and Technology Centre (ARTC) to learn 3D printing, 3D modelling and Arduino coding.

The theme of the innovation camp was "challenges in recycling effort and how they could be reduced, reversing the "disposable economy". Many other teams created solutions to help consumers recycle more easily. However, my team instead focused on the process in a recycling plant. Hence, we created a conveyor belt system integrated with different mechanisms that exploit the unique properties of selected recyclables (e.g. aluminium cans are opaque).

My team amazed the judges and was awarded the "Design Excellence Award".
