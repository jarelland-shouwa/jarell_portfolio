International Exchange Programme to Jakarta, 2023

Visiting a Charged Indonesia factory
Classroom immersion in Bina Bangsa School

I really enjoyed the interaction with the BBS buddies. The conversations we had were insightful and interesting. I am sure I will remember this moment. Moreover, the project collaboration with BBS was impactful on me because I was able to gain new perspectives from the BBS students. I also learnt many other factors, like private transportation, that contributed to the poor air quality in Jakarta other than the usual forest fires in Indonesia.
Enjoying Indonesian cuisine

Concerning Indonesia’s culture and heritage, I was able to appreciate and immerse myself in it. I ate local food for lunch and dinner, saw how BBS operates as a school and understood some history of Indonesia and its provinces. I suppose this trip also had some cultural immersion involved, so as to broaden our views of Indonesia.
Receiving an award for having one of the best team presentations
Presenting our air quality study to judges from SST and BBS
At Bina Bangsa School
Our air quality study results

From the visits to Goto and Charged Indonesia, I have better understood the revolutionary impacts of electric motorcycles and the practices Goto’s sustainability team is developing and is currently implementing to reduce the negative impact of Goto’s ecosystem on Indonesia. Electric motorcycles really can pave the way for reducing the amount of emissions from fueled motorcycles on the roads of Jakarta. If more people use electric motorcycles, the air quality in Jakarta and possibly in the rest of Indonesia, can improve. This is because the most common and popular mode of private transport in Jakarta and probably in the rest of Indonesia is motorcycles.

I understand that Goto wants to be more sustainable and is taking firm actions to enforce its goal, unlike other big companies like the Coca-Cola Company. Nonetheless, the strategies shared by the sustainability team were interesting and shocking for a few that were stated.
