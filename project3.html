As part of the SST S4 Discover programme, I participated in the unique Big Design camp hosted by SUTD and NP.

The theme was "assistive technology in healthcare". My team designed and created a prototype that uses a two-way system, where a camera is used to detect how close the user is to obstacles (via computer vision), and sends commands to a set of vibration motors that vibrate based on the intensity (i.e. depth info from camera feed).
My team was awarded the "Design Excellence Award".
